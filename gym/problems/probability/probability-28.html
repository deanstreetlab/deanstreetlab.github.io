A statistics teacher handed out a set of 10 problems to take home. 5 of them will appear exactly the same in a test.
Tiger has figured out 7 of them but not the others.
What's the probability that Tiger will correctly answer
  1. all 5 problems?
  2. at least 4 of the problems?
a. $$ \left( \frac{ {7\choose 5} }{ { 10\choose 5} } \right) \approx 0.08 $$ b. $$ \left( \frac{ {7\choose 4}{3\choose 1} }{10 \choose 5} \right) + 0.08 \approx 0.5 $$
